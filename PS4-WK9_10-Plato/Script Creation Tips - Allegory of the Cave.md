## After Brainstorming: Ask GenAI

Once your group has ideas, you can use AI tools to help expand them. Try prompts like:

> *"We're writing Scene 2 of a play based on Plato's Allegory of the Cave. Our scene covers the freed prisoner's journey from the cave to the outside world. We've named our character Kira, and she's skeptical but curious. Can you help us draft dialogue for the moment she first sees real objects instead of shadows?"*

Or:

> *"We need stage directions for a moment when a character's eyes adjust to bright light. What physical actions could show this?"*

**Remember:** GenAI is a collaborator, not the author. Start with YOUR ideas, then let AI help you expand them.
